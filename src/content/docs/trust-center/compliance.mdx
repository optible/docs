## SOC 2 Type I

![SOC 2 Type I](/images/soc-2-type-1.png)

Optible is proud to be **SOC 2 Type I compliant**, demonstrating our commitment to maintaining the highest standards of data security, availability, and confidentiality. This certification validates that our systems, processes, and controls meet the rigorous requirements outlined by the **AICPA (American Institute of Certified Public Accountants)**.

As part of our dedication to safeguarding your data, SOC 2 Type I compliance confirms that Optible has implemented robust security practices and controls at a specific point in time.
